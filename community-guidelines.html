Our Community Heartbeat

The values that keep our kitchen warm and welcoming

For Home Cooks

  • Cook with the same love you'd pour into meals for your own family
  • Be transparent about ingredients and preparation methods
  • Honor traditional recipes while making space for innovation

For Buyers

  • Respect the cultural significance behind each dish
  • Provide clear feedback with kindness
  • Understand that home cooking carries unique variations

For Everyone

  • Respect: Honor different culinary traditions and perspectives
  • Honesty: Be truthful in all interactions and transactions
  • Kindness: Assume good intentions in every exchange
  • Safety: Prioritize food safety and personal wellbeing

Our Shared Kitchen

This platform is more than transactions—it's about preserving culinary heritage while building new connections. We resolve conflicts through understanding first, with removal being a last resort for those who repeatedly disregard our community values.
