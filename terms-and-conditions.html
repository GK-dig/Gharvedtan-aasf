  3. Pricing should be fair and reflect the love you put into each dish.

For Buyers

  2. Special dietary requirements must be communicated clearly when ordering.
  3. Respect the cultural significance of each dish you purchase.

For Delivery Partners

  3. You're the final guardian of the food's quality during transit.
